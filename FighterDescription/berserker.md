# Berserker (Blood Red Fighter)

The Berserker is a relentless, dual-wielding melee fighter who thrives on taking damage. With low defensive capabilities but unmatched aggression, the Berserker gets stronger the more damage they sustain, turning pain into pure offensive power.

## Combat Mechanics

### Dual Axes (Melee Attack)
* **Mechanic:** Close-range, devastating chops.
* **Effect:** When an enemy is within range, the Berserker initiates a quick wind-up phase before unleashing a brutal axe swing.
* **Impact:** Successful strikes cause significant screen shake and shower the area in sparks. 

### Blood Rage (Passive / Ultimate State)
* **Mechanic:** The Berserker's signature combat state, fueled by incoming damage.
* **Trigger:** The Berserker possesses a "Rage Meter" that fills up proportionally to the *amount of damage* they take from enemy attacks (not the number of hits). 
* **State Activation:** Once the Rage Meter is completely full, the Berserker automatically enters the **Rage State** for a set duration (approx. 3.3 seconds).
* **Rage Buffs:** While in Rage, the Berserker gains massive enhancements:
  * **Furious Speed:** Movement speed is doubled (x2.0 multiplier).
  * **Frenzied Strikes:** Axe attack cooldown is reduced, allowing for significantly faster swings (x1.1 attack speed).
  * **Brutal Damage:** Axe attacks deal vastly increased damage (x1.8 multiplier).
  * **Lifesteal (Vampirism):** During Rage, every successful axe strike heals the Berserker for a massive 80% of the damage dealt.
* **Visuals:** The Berserker glows crimson red, leaves aggressive motion trails, and emits a fiery aura. Impacts generate heavier screen shake and crimson sparks.

### Relentless Pursuit (Arena Physics)
* **Mechanic:** Unlike standard fighters who naturally deflect away from walls upon impact, the Berserker employs aggressive pathfinding.
* **Effect:** Whenever the Berserker bounces off an arena wall, their trajectory immediately snaps directly toward the opponent, ensuring they never retreat.
* **Rage Bounce:** While in the Rage State, this auto-lock is even tighter. The "rebounce away distance" is minimized, allowing the Berserker to instantly re-engage their target without losing momentum.
