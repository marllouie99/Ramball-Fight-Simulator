# Cronos - Combat Mechanics

Cronos is a **Time-Manipulating Close-Combat Fighter** who dominates the battlefield by controlling the flow of time. Wielding a sharp crescent blade and deploying time-stopping zones, Cronos excels at locking down opponents and punishing them with rapid strikes.

## Core Mechanics
* **Counter-Stop (Passive):** Cronos has a built-in defense mechanism that disrupts attackers. Whenever Cronos takes damage, there is a chance to instantly inflict a "Time Stop" on the attacker. This completely freezes the enemy's movement, rotation, and aiming for a short duration, leaving them vulnerable to counterattacks.
* **Basic Attack (Crescent Blade):** A sharp, close-range melee slash using a glowing cyan crescent blade. This attack features a **Double Strike** window, allowing Cronos to follow up a first swing with a rapid second strike if executed quickly. The slashes leave behind sharp, diamond-shaped cyan particle effects.

## Active Abilities

### Time Sphere (Skill 1)
* **Mechanic:** A localized zone of temporal distortion deployed on the battlefield.
* **Effect:** Cronos drops a Time Stop Sphere at a designated location. Any enemies and hostile projectiles that enter or are caught inside the sphere are completely frozen in time for the duration of the effect.
* **Empowered State:** The Time Sphere does not hinder Cronos. Instead, while inside their own Time Sphere, Cronos gains significantly increased movement speed. This allows them to easily reposition, rapidly strike frozen targets, or utilize the sphere's boundaries to bounce with enhanced force.
