# Gojo Satoru

**Category:** Anime
**Color:** Light Cyan (`#E0FFFF`)
**Role:** Crowd Control, Burst Mage

## Lore / Background
The strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer, Gojo Satoru has brought his overwhelming power into the Circle Mini-Battle arena. With the Six Eyes and the Limitless Cursed Technique, he controls space at an atomic level, making him nearly untouchable while dishing out catastrophic damage.

## Abilities

### 🛡️ Passive: Limitless (Infinity)
An absolute barrier of spatial distortion surrounds Gojo. Every few seconds, the first incoming attack to enter his radius will have its speed reduced to 0 and its damage completely nullified. You will see an `INFINITY` text pop up when this triggers.

### 🔫 Basic Attack: Cursed Technique Lapse - Blue
Instead of normal bullets, Gojo fires small orbs of condensing space. When these hit an enemy or expire, they act as miniature black holes, dragging enemies inward to group them up for devastating combo attacks.

### 🔴 Skill 1 (Passive trigger): Cursed Technique Reversal - Red
When an enemy gets too close, Gojo will automatically snap his fingers and trigger a massive repulsion wave. This deals heavy damage and flings enemies far away, resetting his personal space.

### 🟣 Skill 2 (Active state): Hollow Purple
Gojo's ultimate offensive technique. He stops moving to channel the technique, merging Red and Blue. After a short wind-up, he fires a massive, slow-moving purple orb that pierces through all enemies and completely deletes any enemy projectiles caught in its path.

### 🌌 Ultimate: Domain Expansion - Unlimited Void
Gojo expands his domain across the entire arena. The screen shakes and colors invert. For the duration of the domain, the sheer amount of information overloads the brains of all enemies on the map, rendering them completely paralyzed and unable to act.
