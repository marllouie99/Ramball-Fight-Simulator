# Musashi - Combat Mechanics

Musashi is a **Dual Sword Stance Fighter** who wields a Katana and a Wakizashi. His playstyle revolves around automatically cycling through five distinct elemental stances, each granting unique passive benefits and modifying his combat flow. He excels at rapid strikes, counter-attacks, and devastating multi-hit combos.

## Core Mechanics
* **Dual Wielding:** Attacks combine the damage of his Katana and Wakizashi. 
* **Stance Cycling:** Musashi automatically cycles through 5 elemental stances (Earth, Water, Fire, Wind, Void). Each stance lasts for 5 seconds (300 frames) before transitioning to the next.

## Stances
* **Earth Stance (Brown):** Focuses on defense. Modifies damage taken (simulating parries) and provides a slight increase to movement speed.
* **Water Stance (Blue):** Focuses on mobility. Significantly increases movement speed and dodge distance. Musashi starts the battle in this stance.
* **Fire Stance (Orange):** Focuses on aggression. Increases damage dealt by his sword strikes, but makes him slightly more vulnerable, increasing the damage he takes.
* **Wind Stance (Green):** Focuses on anti-ranged combat. Automatically deflects and destroys enemy projectiles that enter a radius around him.
* **Void Stance (Purple):** Focuses on evasion and disruption. Upon entering this stance, Musashi briefly freezes time for nearby enemies ("VOID FREEZE"). While active, he has a 100% chance to dodge incoming attacks (unless stunned or time-stopped).

## Active Abilities

### Preemptive Strike (Counter Stance)
* **Mechanic:** Musashi occasionally enters a defensive posture when an enemy is nearby. 
* **Effect:** If attacked during this short window, he negates the incoming damage, teleports directly behind the attacker (leaving a trail of afterimages), and delivers a guaranteed counter-attack that causes screen shake.

### Niten Strike
* **Mechanic:** A heavy, two-part precision strike.
* **Effect:** Initiates with a quick, angled Wakizashi slash for light damage and guard breaking, followed shortly by a heavy Katana slash that deals significant damage, high knockback, and leaves the opponent reeling.

### Phantom Flurry
* **Mechanic:** A devastating "Omnislash" style ultimate attack.
* **Effect:** Musashi teleports to his target and unleashes 5 rapid, teleporting slashes. 
* **Stance Mastery:** During the flurry, Musashi cycles through all 5 stances sequentially for each hit, dynamically changing the color of his weapon auras and slash effects. 
* **Visuals:** The attack features dramatic hit-pauses, dense trails of afterimages, screen shakes, and ends with a lingering smoke trail as he returns to his true stance.
