# Zeus - Combat Mechanics

Zeus is a **Spell Caster** who wields a physical bolt of lightning as his weapon. He excels at devastating burst damage and crowd control through electrical attacks, but has low health and mobility. His playstyle revolves around chaining lightning attacks between enemies and controlling the battlefield with powerful area-of-effect abilities.

## Core Mechanics
* **Lightning Weapon:** Zeus holds a physical bolt of lightning in his hand, serving as his primary weapon.
* **Chain Lightning:** His attacks can bounce between multiple enemies and even off walls, maximizing damage potential.
* **Debuff Application:** Lightning attacks apply status effects like "stun," "paralyze," or "static" that slow down or temporarily disable enemies.

## Active Abilities

### Chain Lightning (Basic Attack)
* **Mechanic:** Zeus hurls a bolt of electricity that jumps from one enemy to another.
* **Effect:** The lightning bolt deals damage to the initial target, then automatically chains to nearby enemies within range.
* **Wall Bouncing:** If no enemies are in range, the lightning can bounce off arena walls, potentially finding new targets or extending the chain.
* **Debuffs:** Each lightning strike has a chance to apply:
  * **Stun:** Briefly immobilizes the target
  * **Paralyze:** Reduces the target's movement and attack speed
  * **Static:** Causes the target to take increased damage from subsequent lightning attacks
* **Visuals:** Crackling electrical arcs between targets, with bright flashes and electrical particle effects.

### Aegis Shield (Passive Skill)
* **Mechanic:** A defensive barrier that surrounds Zeus when enemies approach.
* **Effect:** When an enemy attacks Zeus at close range (melee attack), the Aegis Shield automatically activates.
* **Shock Response:** The shield delivers a powerful electrical shock to the attacker, dealing damage and applying a strong paralyze debuff.
* **Cooldown:** The shield has a brief cooldown between activations to prevent permanent protection.
* **Visuals:** A visible electrical barrier aura around Zeus that intensifies when struck, accompanied by a dramatic shock effect on the attacker.

### Storm (Ultimate Skill)
* **Mechanic:** A map-wide electrical storm that strikes every enemy on the battlefield.
* **Effect:** Zeus summons a massive storm that covers the entire arena, striking all enemies regardless of their position or cover.
* **Damage:** Each enemy takes significant lightning damage from multiple strikes over the duration of the storm.
* **Debuffs:** Storm strikes apply heavy static and paralyze debuffs to all affected enemies.
* **Global Reach:** This ultimate cannot be dodged or avoided by hiding behind obstacles or at the arena edges.
* **Visuals:** The entire arena darkens with storm clouds, accompanied by dramatic lightning strikes across the battlefield, screen flashes, and thunderous audio effects.

## Visual Design
* **Static Electricity:** Zeus is constantly surrounded by crackling static electricity, creating a visible electrical aura.
* **Lightning Weapon:** His weapon is a literal physical bolt of lightning held in his hand, pulsing with energy.
* **Color Scheme:** Predominantly electric blue and bright yellow/white for lightning effects, with darker storm clouds for his ultimate.
* **Effects:** Electrical trails, particle effects, and dramatic lighting accompany all his abilities.
