# Hydra (The Unstoppable Juggernaut)

Hydra is an incredibly tanky bruiser with an absurd health pool (5x normal) but very low innate damage. He relies on surviving massive amounts of punishment while stealing the weapons of those foolish enough to attack him in melee combat.

## Passive 1: Endless Regrowth
Hydra has a chance to instantly destroy (cut) incoming enemy projectiles before they can hit him.

## Passive 2: Revenge Drive
As Hydra takes damage, his revenge meter fills. Once it reaches 100%, he unleashes a massive shockwave that damages all nearby enemies.

## Weapon Steal Mechanic
When a fighter strikes Hydra with a physical weapon, Hydra copies that weapon and uses it for his own basic attacks for a few seconds.

## Active Skill: The Overdrive
Hydra enters an overdrive state, glowing intensely and becoming completely immune to all crowd control effects (stuns, knockbacks, pulls).
