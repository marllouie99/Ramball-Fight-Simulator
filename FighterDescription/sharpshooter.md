# Sharpshooter / Crimson Sniper (Red Fighter)

The Sharpshooter (formerly known as the Crimson Sniper) is a high-stakes, precision-based ranged fighter. Unlike other fighters who spray projectiles wildly, the Sharpshooter relies on a limited magazine, a deadly "Execute" mechanic on their final bullet, and a target-locking aim system to systematically take down their opponents.

## Combat Mechanics

### Magazine & Reload System
* **Mechanic:** Ammo management.
* **Effect:** The Sharpshooter uses a magazine of bullets (typically 4). Each shot consumes a bullet. Once the magazine is empty, the fighter is forced to automatically reload.
* **Vulnerability:** While reloading, the Sharpshooter cannot fire and their movement speed is drastically reduced (to 20% of normal), making them highly vulnerable to being rushed down.

### Target Lock Aiming
* **Mechanic:** Smooth, constant tracking.
* **Effect:** Instead of spinning continuously like a standard fighter, the Sharpshooter smoothly and continuously rotates to keep their weapon trained directly on the opponent's position, ensuring they are always ready to fire when the target is aligned.

### Tension Aura & Focus State
* **Mechanic:** Preparation for the final shot.
* **Effect:** When the Sharpshooter drops to their last 2 bullets, a distinct auditory cue plays and a dark visual "Tension Aura" begins to build around them. 
* **Focus State:** When they are down to their absolute **last bullet**, the Sharpshooter enters a "Focus State." Their movement drops to a near standstill (5% speed) and they emit "FOCUS..." visual prompts, preparing for the kill.

### The Execute (Enhanced Last Bullet)
* **Mechanic:** Devastating final shot.
* **Trigger:** Firing the very last bullet in the magazine.
* **Effect:** The final shot requires a brief "CHARGING..." wind-up phase. Once fired, it unleashes an **Enhanced Execute Bullet**.
* **Impact:** This shot travels 50% faster, deals massive bonus damage (2.5x base damage), causes massive physical recoil to the Sharpshooter, and triggers a dramatic global screen shake with an "EXECUTE!" pop-up text.
* **Electrification:** The execute bullet also applies an "Electrified" debuff on hit, shocking the target for a set duration.
