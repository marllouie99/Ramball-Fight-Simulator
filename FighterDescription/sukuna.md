# Ryomen Sukuna

**Category:** Anime
**Color:** Dark Crimson (`#8B0000`)
**Role:** Melee Skirmisher, Executioner

## Lore / Background
The undisputed King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna has awakened to reclaim his throne. Armed with unmatched malice, invisible slicing techniques, and devastating hellfire, he thrives in pure slaughter. He is uniquely equipped to dismantle anyone who claims to be the "strongest."

## Abilities

### 🩸 Passive: Reverse Cursed Technique
Sukuna's malice keeps him tethered to life. When his health drops below 30%, or upon taking a fatal hit, he instantly consumes his stored energy to regenerate a massive chunk of HP. A `REVERSE` text pops up when this triggers (Cooldown: 20 seconds).

### ⚔️ Basic Attack: Dismantle & Cursed Martial Arts
Sukuna dynamically adapts his basic attack based on distance to target:
- **Dismantle (Long Distance)**: Sukuna swipes his fingers, sending out a grid of less visible flying slashes in a target direction.
- **Cursed Martial Arts (Close Distance)**: Sukuna engages in brutal, high-speed hand-to-hand combat. Every third or fourth hit in his basic attack string sends out a short-range Dismantle slash, dealing bonus damage to enemies slightly out of his melee range.

### 🩸 Skill 1: Phantom Flurry + Cleave
A targeted close-range physical grab or strike combined with a short-range Dismantle slash wave, dealing bonus damage to enemies slightly out of his melee range.

### 🔥 Skill 2: Furnace (Divine Flame / Fuga)
Sukuna claps his hands together and draws a bow made of fire, launching a devastating flaming arrow. A slow-charging but massive single-target nuke. It creates a thermobaric explosion upon impact, dealing heavy splash damage to enemies near the primary target.

### 💀 Ultimate: Domain Expansion - Malevolent Shrine
Sukuna forms his hand sign, summoning his demonic shrine and locking a massive radius of the battlefield into his Domain. Instead of trapping people in a barrier, it creates a massive, open-air zone of death. For the next several seconds, any enemy standing inside the radius is continuously hit by a rapid, unavoidable barrage of Cleaves and Dismantles. The damage ramps up the longer enemies stay inside the zone.
