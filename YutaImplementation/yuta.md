# Yuta Okkotsu

**Category:** Anime
**Color:** Soft Silver/White (`#EEEEEE`)
**Role:** Hybrid Melee/Ranged, Summoner

## Lore / Background
One of the Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerers, Yuta Okkotsu joins the arena alongside his childhood friend turned Special Grade Vengeful Cursed Spirit, Rika Orimoto. With immense cursed energy and the ability to copy cursed techniques, Yuta seamlessly blends deadly close-quarters katana strikes with overwhelming copied abilities.

## Abilities

### 🛡️ Passive: Reverse Cursed Technique (RCT)
Due to his vast reserves of cursed energy, Yuta constantly outputs Reverse Cursed Technique to passively regenerate his health over time. As long as he is alive, his HP will steadily recover.

### ⚔️ Basic Attack: Katana Melee
When enemies get too close, Yuta unleashes sweeping katana strikes. His melee attacks cover a wide arc and deal high burst damage with strong knockback, making him extremely dangerous in close quarters.

### 🔄 Skill 1 (Cycling Ranged): Copied Techniques
When attacking from a distance, Yuta will cycle through the cursed techniques he has copied:
- **Cursed Speech:** Yuta fires an expanding shockwave. Enemies caught in its radius are completely frozen in time by the command "DON'T MOVE!".
- **Thin Ice Breaker:** A devastating spatial distortion projectile that shatters space itself. It moves incredibly fast and blasts enemies backwards with massive force upon impact.

### 👹 Skill 2 (Summon): Rika Orimoto
When Yuta's Domain Expansion activates, Rika is fully manifested on the battlefield. Rika acts as an autonomous AI companion, relentlessly chasing down enemies and mauling them with heavy damage while Yuta is free to move and attack independently.

### 🌌 Ultimate: Domain Expansion - Authentic Mutual Love
When surrounded by enemies and ready, Yuta expands his domain, plunging the arena into a dark pulsating void filled with scattered katanas. During the domain's duration, Yuta's ability cooldowns are drastically reduced, allowing him to spam his copied techniques endlessly while Rika provides constant pressure.
